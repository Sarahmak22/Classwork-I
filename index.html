I learnt that the emphasis tag is used to make an emphasis on the word. It shouldn't be used instead of italics tag because this tag is used to make a word appear in italics. Another tag I learnt about is the strong tag which can sometimes be confused with the bold tag. However, it is used to make a text appear important to the user.
The break tag is used when you want a break in your paragraph or want to start on a new line. Strike through tag is used to strike through a word. The example that came to mind was for slashed prices during sales.
